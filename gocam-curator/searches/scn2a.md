# Search: SCN2A

**Species:** Mus musculus (taxon:10090)  
**Date:** 2026-03-17 12:50

**Protein name:** Sodium channel protein type 2 subunit alpha  
**Gene names:** Scn2a / Scn2a1

## Function

Mediates the voltage-dependent sodium ion permeability of excitable membranes. Assuming opened or closed conformations in response to the voltage difference across the membrane, the protein forms a sodium-selective channel through which Na(+) ions may pass in accordance with their electrochemical gradient (PubMed:10827969, PubMed:11166117, PubMed:28137877). Implicated in the regulation of hippocampal replay occurring within sharp wave ripples (SPW-R) important for memory (PubMed:29867081)
